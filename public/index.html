My name is Mabel and I am a freelance fullstack web developer. I started coding because I wanted a career that was equaling challenging as well as rewarding. As someone who benefits from technology on a daily basis, I also want to have an input in the future of this industry.

When I am not coding, I enjoy trying new restaurants, traveling to new places and exploring my favorite place in the world: NYC!

I would love to link up with an organization where I can contribute to their goals and work with others who will help me continue to grow as a developer.
